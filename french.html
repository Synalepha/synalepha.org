Three lesson sparks

Novice · Interpretive

Which forecast fits?

Read or hear brief forecasts from two Francophone locations. Match clothing and plans to each, then choose your preference.

Intermediate · Interpersonal

Plan a local outing

Use two authentic event listings. Partners exchange constraints, negotiate, and settle on one plan.

Intermediate · Presentational

A voice from the Francophonie

Introduce a creator or community initiative using sources from that person or community, then explain why it matters.

Regional and inclusive-language notes

Make the source visible

Name the community and setting behind audio or text. Treat accents and regional terms as ordinary linguistic variation, not obstacles or comic curiosities.

Give inclusive forms context

Feminization, neutral wording, and forms such as the midpoint vary by institution and community. Explain where a form appears and prioritize people’s own terms.
