Input

Comprehensible and meaningful

Learners need messages they can understand. Make the topic worth understanding, support meaning with context or visuals, and ask for a response that shows what was understood.

Try: Give learners a reason to select, rank, match, or react to information.

ACTFL guiding principles
Attention

Structured input

Input can be arranged so learners attend to a form while processing meaning. Begin with referential items that have an answer grounded in the input, then use affective items that connect meaning to learners.

Try: Control competing cues and require no production of the target form during input processing.

ERIC search: processing instruction
Communication

Three communicative modes

Interpretive, interpersonal, and presentational communication call for different kinds of performance. A coherent lesson can let input supply content for interaction and a later product.

Try: Ask what learners understand, negotiate, and share—not merely which technology they use.

Evidence

Observable achievement

Assessment descriptions are more useful when they point to features visible in the specific performance. Effort, participation, preparation, and timeliness may matter, but they should not alter a description of demonstrated achievement.

Try: Complete the sentence “A teacher or learner can point to…” before writing level descriptors.

Evidence

Formative assessment

Evidence is formative when it changes what teachers or learners do next. Keep checks closely tied to the outcome and small enough to act on during learning.

Try: Decide in advance what evidence will trigger reteaching, more practice, source verification, or an extension.

Carnegie Mellon: formative and summative assessment
Growth

Feedback and fresh evidence

Useful feedback points toward the goal, names evidence in the work, and offers a manageable next move. A credible reassessment asks for fresh evidence of the same outcome after feedback and further practice.

Try: Prioritize one or two changes, protect learner ownership of revision, and state readiness conditions.
