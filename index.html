About

Synalepha — the joining together of separate threads into a meaningful whole — is a space for thinking deeply about what matters.

We explore the structures that hold us together and the forces that pull us apart. From the psychology of human connection to the philosophy of meaning, from data-driven research to reflective writing, this is a place for ideas that matter.
