About

The answer isn't more therapy.
It's community.

Men's mental health is in crisis. Suicide rates are 4× higher than women's. 15% of young men have no close friends. 40% have never spoken to anyone about their mental health.

The loneliness epidemic isn't abstract — it's real, and it's killing men quietly.

We believe the answer isn't more clinical therapy (though that has its place). The answer is community. Real conversations. Real presence. Men sitting together, being honest about what's hard, and finding strength in shared experience.

This isn't a support group run by a therapist. It's a group of men supporting men — because sometimes what you need isn't a professional, it's a brother.
