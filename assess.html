Assessment review

Inspect evidence without outsourcing judgment.

Load a Lesson Studio JSON file, add one plain-text learner response, and receive transparent evidence indicators plus an editable feedback draft.

1. Load the lesson

The lesson export supplies the language, outcome, evidence plan, and editable rubric. No student name or identifier is needed.

No rubric loaded.

Teacher plans carried into analysis

2. Add one response

Privacy: remove names and sensitive information. When you analyze, text is sent to the site’s same-origin function, processed in memory, and returned with no-store headers. Synalepha does not intentionally persist it.

Up to 40 KB. PDF, DOCX, image, audio, and video are rejected rather than silently ignored.

What this can do

  • Count visible evidence
  • Check direct outcome and rubric wording overlap
  • Flag language and evidence signals for inspection
  • Draft feedback that remains editable

What it cannot do

  • Assign a defensible grade
  • Verify accuracy, meaning, originality, proficiency, or culture
  • Process handwriting, audio, images, PDFs, or word-processing files
  • Replace teacher review
