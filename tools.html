A connected workflow

The tools can stand alone, but they work best as one teacher-controlled sequence.

  1. Find: choose a source by language, mode, range, and access.
  2. Plan: turn that source into meaning-focused input, purposeful communication, and observable evidence.
  3. Review: compare a de-identified response with the lesson outcome and rubric indicators, then revise the draft feedback yourself.

Synalepha does not assign grades or replace professional judgment. Review source permissions, language accuracy, accessibility, culture, and all feedback before use.
